What We Treat

Comprehensive Urologic Services

Our experienced team diagnoses and treats a wide range of urological conditions with precision and care.

General Urology
Comprehensive evaluation and treatment of urinary tract conditions affecting men, women, and children.
Prostate Care
Prostate cancer screening, BPH evaluation, and minimally invasive treatment options.
Kidney Conditions
Diagnosis and management of kidney stones, kidney disease, and related urinary disorders.
Men's Health
Specialized care for male urological concerns including erectile dysfunction, male infertility, and more.
Bladder Disorders
Treatment of overactive bladder, incontinence, bladder infections, and interstitial cystitis.
Outpatient Procedures
Surgical procedures at San Gabriel Valley Surgical Center and Queen of the Valley Hospital, West Covina.
